Формат файла учета трафика:<traffic> = <trafficCount: uint16><trafficRecs: 1...n><trafficRecs: 1...n> = <trafficRec> ... <trafficRec>
<trafficRec> = routeIdLength: uint16><routeId: routeIdLength bytes>        <per_min: uint32> <per_hour: uint32> <per_day: uint32> <per_month: uint32>         <year: uint8> <month: uint8> <day: uint8> <hour: uint8> <min: uint8>
